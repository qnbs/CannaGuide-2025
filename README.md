## üèÅ Getting Started

No installation or setup is required. The application runs entirely in your web browser.

1.  **Explore Strains:** Start in the **Strains** view to browse the database. Use the filters and search bar to find a strain that interests you, and save it to your favorites with the heart icon.
2.  **Start a Grow:** From a strain's detail page, click "Start Growing" to configure your initial setup and add the new plant to your **Plants** dashboard.
3.  **Manage Your Plants:** The **Plants** view is your main dashboard. The simulation progresses automatically. Click on a plant to inspect its detailed status, log actions in its journal, and complete tasks to keep it healthy and stress-free.
4.  **Learn and Plan:** Use the **Knowledge** view to follow the step-by-step guide and the **Equipment** view to plan your real-world setup with the AI configurator and calculators.
5.  **Use the Command Palette:** For quick access, press `Cmd/Ctrl + K` anytime to jump between sections or perform actions instantly.
